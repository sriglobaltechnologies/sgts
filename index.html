About Us

Established in 1997, Sri Global Technologies and Services in Bhimavaram offers 28 years of expertise in laptop solutions. We provide precision chip-level repairs, seamless system installations, and premium hardware, ensuring top-notch digital experiences.

Why Choose Sri Global Technologies and Services?

  • Decades of Trust: With over two decades in the industry, our longevity speaks volumes about our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
  • Expert Technicians: Our team comprises highly skilled and experienced technicians proficient in the latest diagnostic and repair techniques.
  • Customer-Centric Approach: We prioritize understanding your unique needs and provide tailored solutions with transparent communication and reliable support.
  • Comprehensive Solutions: From intricate hardware repairs to seamless software installations and premium hardware sales, we offer a holistic approach to laptop care.
  • Conveniently Located: Situated in a central area of Bhimavaram (Opposite Divya Fancy Store, Police Bomma Center), we are easily accessible for all your laptop needs.

At Sri Global Technologies and Services, we are dedicated to extending the life and enhancing the performance of your laptops. Trust us to be your reliable partner in navigating the ever-evolving world of technology.
